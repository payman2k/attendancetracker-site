Support

Find answers to common questions below, or contact us directly for help.

Getting Started

How do I log in for the first time?

The app comes with sample data including default staff PINs. Use PIN 1234 (Admin), 2468 (Teacher), or 3691 (Assistant) to log in. We recommend changing these immediately after your first login.

How do I access management features?

Student, Classroom, and Staff management require admin access. Tap any management tab in the sidebar, then enter the admin password when prompted. The default admin password is admin123 -- change it immediately under Staff Management.

Daily Use

How do I check in a student?

Go to Check In/Out, tap the student's card, then choose Check In from the action menu. You can also mark students as Late, Excused, or Absent.

How do I check out a student?

Tap a checked-in student's card and choose Check Out. The checkout time is recorded in the attendance history.

Can I filter by classroom?

Yes. On the Check In/Out screen, use the All Classrooms dropdown at the top to filter students by their assigned classroom.

Reports

How do I view attendance history?

Go to Attendance History (admin access required). You can filter by student, date range (Today, Week, Month, or Custom), and see check-in/out times for each record.

How are attendance percentages calculated?

Present, Late, and Excused all count as "attended." Only Absent counts against the attendance percentage.

Security

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data stays on your iPad. Staff PINs and passwords are hashed with SHA-256 and stored in the iOS Keychain. The app auto-locks after inactivity.

What happens if I uninstall the app?

All data is permanently deleted when the app is uninstalled. Data is included in standard iPad backups.

Troubleshooting

The app logged me out unexpectedly

The app has an auto-logout feature that activates after a period of inactivity. This is a security feature for shared devices. Simply log in again with your PIN.

I forgot my PIN

Contact your administrator. They can reset your PIN from the Staff Management screen.
