Feature Roadmap

We're building Attendance Tracker into a complete childcare management platform. Here's what's ahead — and we'd love your input on what to prioritize.

Phase 1 Cloud Sync & Export

Goal: Break out of single-device limitation

  • ☁️

    Cloud Backup & Multi-Device Sync

    Securely sync your data across multiple iPads so your whole team stays up to date in real time.

  • 📤

    CSV & PDF Report Export

    Download attendance reports as CSV or PDF for record-keeping, audits, and sharing with administrators.

  • 📋

    Enhanced Student Profiles

    Add date of birth, allergies, emergency contacts, parent info, and photos to each student's record.

  • 📝

    Check-In / Check-Out Notes

    Staff can add notes when checking students in or out — record pickup authorizations, health notes, and more.

Phase 2 Family Connection

Goal: Bridge the gap between center and home

  • 📰

    Daily Activity Reports

    Log meals, naps, activities, and mood throughout the day. Parents see a full summary of their child's day.

  • 📸

    Photo Capture & Sharing

    Teachers snap photos during the day and share them securely with families.

  • 👨‍👩‍👧

    Parent Portal

    A dedicated web portal where parents can view daily reports, attendance history, and their child's profile.

  • 💬

    In-App Messaging

    Direct and group messaging between staff and parents — announcements, updates, and private conversations.

Phase 3 Compliance & Operations

Goal: Handle the operational complexity of running a center

  • 🚨

    Staff-to-Child Ratio Monitoring

    Set ratio requirements per room and get real-time alerts when ratios are out of compliance.

  • 🩺

    Health Checks

    Log temperatures and illness symptoms at check-in. Flag concerns and maintain health records.

  • 😴

    Sleep & Nap Tracking

    Log nap start/end times for infants and toddlers to meet licensing requirements.

  • 📋

    Behavior Incident Tracking

    Document incidents, categorize by type, and notify parents with secure, private reports.

  • 📅

    Daily Schedules

    Build structured daily routines per classroom — activities, meals, rest, and transitions.

Phase 4 Enrollment & Billing

Goal: Handle the business side of childcare

  • 📝

    Digital Enrollment Forms & E-Signatures

    Accept applications online, collect documents, and gather legally-binding e-signatures — no paper needed.

  • 📋

    Waitlist Management

    Track prospective families, manage inquiries, and automatically admit students when spots open.

  • 💳

    Tuition Billing & Invoicing

    Generate invoices, set up billing plans, and send receipts — all from one place.

  • 💰

    Online Payment Processing

    Accept online payments and autopay so you get paid on time without chasing families.

  • 📊

    Expense Tracking

    Track center-level expenses to understand your true operating costs and manage budgets.

Phase 5 Advanced Features

Goal: Enterprise-ready childcare management

  • 📚

    Lesson Planning & Curriculum

    Create and share lesson plans with templates, activities, supplies lists, and state standards alignment.

  • 🗓️

    Staff Shift Scheduling

    Schedule shifts, manage coverage, and ensure you always have the right staff-to-child ratios.

  • 🏢

    Multi-Location Management

    Manage multiple centers from one account with cross-location visibility and reporting.

  • 📒

    QuickBooks Integration

    Export billing data directly to QuickBooks Online for seamless accounting and tax prep.

  • 📱

    Contactless QR Code Check-In

    Families scan a QR code at drop-off and pick-up for fast, touchless attendance tracking.

  • 🍎

    CACFP Food Program Tracking

    Track meal counts and generate CACFP reports for food program compliance and reimbursement.
