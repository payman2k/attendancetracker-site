Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2, 2026

Overview

Attendance Tracker ("the App") is designed to help preschools and childcare centers track student attendance. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially the children whose information may be managed through this app.

Data Collection

The App collects and stores the following information locally on your iPad device:

  • Student information: First name, last name, classroom assignment, and active status
  • Attendance records: Check-in/check-out times, attendance status (present, absent, late, excused), date, and the staff member who recorded the entry
  • Staff information: First name, last name, role, and authentication credentials (stored securely in the iOS Keychain)
  • Classroom information: Classroom names and capacity

Data Storage

All data is stored exclusively on your local device. The App does not transmit any data to external servers, cloud services, or third parties. Staff credentials (PINs and passwords) are hashed using SHA-256 and stored in the iOS Keychain, Apple's secure credential storage system.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties. No data leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App does not use cookies or web tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

The App is designed for use by authorized staff members at educational institutions. While the App manages information about children (names and attendance), it is operated exclusively by adult staff members. The App does not collect information directly from children, does not require internet access, and does not share any child data externally.

Data Retention

All data persists on the device until manually deleted by an administrator through the App's management features, or until the App is uninstalled from the device. Uninstalling the App permanently removes all stored data.

Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • PIN-based authentication for all staff access
  • SHA-256 hashed credentials stored in the iOS Keychain
  • Role-based access controls (Admin, Teacher, Assistant)
  • Automatic logout after configurable inactivity period
  • Admin password required for management operations

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data. You can:

  • View all stored data through the App's interface
  • Edit or delete any student, staff, or attendance records
  • Remove all data by uninstalling the App

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
